Admission Form - Instructions

List of Supporting Documents:
Please bring the following self-attested documents along with original documents when you come for admission.

  1. Two recent passport size photographs of the student and Family group photograph (Mother, Father of child and Child).
  2. True Copy of the Birth Certificate. In case of certificates in languages other than English, please submit a notarized copy of the certificate translated in English.
  3. True copy of Residence proof as mentioned below:
    Residence proof should strictly be in the name of the father,mother or grandparents of the child seeking admission.
    Any ONE of the following documents can be produced as proof of residence:
    1. Passport (Recent)
    2. Electricity Bill(Recent)
    3. Telephone Bill (Land Line)
    4. Unique I.D. (Aadhar) Card
    5. Bank statement for the current month
    6. Registered Agreement Copy (Purchase or Rented) #
    7. # If a copy of rent agreement is attached as the proof of residence, then it should be accompanied by one more address proof, out of the options given above.
      Please Note - Ration Card Will NOT be accepted as proof of residence.
    1. Medical Certificate provided in the printout of online Admission Form has to be completely filled, signed and stamped by a registered medical practitioner.
    2. Parents from Reserved Category, who want the caste to be entered in the school register should submit the Caste Certificate issued by the concerned Government Authorities in the name of the student.
    3. Previous year's report card if applicable.

      Admission Process for Nursery
      • Eligibility : 3 years complete as on 30th September 2018.
      • Seats will be reserved for Siblings of our existing students who fall in the right age group and who have completed the admission process as per schedule given above.
      • Cousins Will Not be considered as siblings.
      • Following this all other seats will be filled as per Random Selection by Lottery system generated by computer program.
      • Parents are requested Not to consider the seat as allotted till the Admission confirmation notification is received.
